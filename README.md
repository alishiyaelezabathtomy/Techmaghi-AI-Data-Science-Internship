## 📌 About the Internship
This internship focused on applying Artificial Intelligence and Data Science techniques to real-world business problems. I worked on building machine learning and deep learning models, performing data analysis, and developing AI-based solutions for text, image, and business datasets.

The internship provided hands-on experience in the complete data science workflow, including data preprocessing, model development, evaluation, and visualization.
